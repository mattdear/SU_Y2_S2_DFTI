About HitTastic!

The UK's premier music search site

HitTastic! was founded five years ago by Alex Martin in a Sussex bedroom in order to provide an online search facility for music. The site was highly successful and by linking up with providers of music expanded to the search and buy site that you see today. HitTastic! now has its own premises at the Nyefield Business Park, Nyefield, West Sussex.
